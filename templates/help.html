Help Center

Welcome to the GHOSTCODE Help Center. Here you'll find guidance on how to use our steganography tools and answers to frequently asked questions.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Choose your preferred steganography method (Text, Audio, Image, or Video).
  2. Upload the file you want to use as a carrier for your hidden message.
  3. Enter your secret message and a private key.
  4. Click the "Hide" button to process your file.
  5. Download the resulting file, which now contains your hidden message.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is steganography?

A: Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video.

Q: Is GHOSTCODE secure?

A: Yes, GHOSTCODE uses advanced algorithms to ensure that your hidden messages are secure and undetectable by casual observation.

Q: What file types can I use with GHOSTCODE?

A: GHOSTCODE supports .txt files for text steganography, .wav files for audio and .png files for images

Q: Why do I need a private key?

A: The private key adds an extra layer of security. Only someone with both the steganographic file and the correct private key can extract the hidden message.

Q: How can I extract a hidden message?

A: We're working on an extraction feature. For now, GHOSTCODE focuses on hiding messages. Stay tuned for updates!
